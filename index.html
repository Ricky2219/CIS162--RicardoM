What Is Engineering?

Engineering is the application of science and mathematics to solve problems. Engineers figure out how things work and find practical uses for scientific discoveries.


Scientists and inventors often get the credit for innovations that advance the human condition, but it is engineers who are instrumental in making those innovations.
